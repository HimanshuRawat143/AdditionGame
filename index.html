The Mathematical Trick Revealed!

Here's how the trick works:

  1. The answer is calculated immediately after you enter the first row
  2. To get the answer:
    • Take the digits from the first row as is
    • Subtract 2 from the rightmost digit
    • Add a new digit "2" at the leftmost position
  3. For system-generated rows (rows 3 and 5):
    • Each digit + the corresponding digit in the previous row must sum to 9

It's a clever mathematical trick that works every time!
